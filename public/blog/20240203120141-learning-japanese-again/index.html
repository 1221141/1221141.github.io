Learning Japanese Again

2024-02-03T12:01:41

I decided to start learning Japanese again. Initially I thought about learning Korean, since I started to have interest in Korean culture and series, but the sounds are too hard, and the grammar is hard as well. Since I had studied previously and have more knowledge about Japanese culture I thought that would be easier to learn.
Also there is a lot of material to learn Japanese online in English, but not a lot in Korean. Also Korean grammar is somewhat similar to Japanese, and there are several statements online that the number of material to learn Korean in Japanese is significantly higher.
So the current plan is to learn Japanese and when I'm good enough try to learn Korean from it. Meanwhile I will try to get more Korean input and learn Hangul, their writing system.
So far I memorized most of the hiragana, katakana is the one I have more difficult.
